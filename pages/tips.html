Tips for Living like a Local

Currency

Money is a safety net when traveling, so it is important to make sure you have what you need while visiting our island. Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Electronics Safety

We know that no matter where we travel, we rely on our portable electronics for various purposes; whether business or personal, just for fun or in case of emergencies. Keeping our items charged is a modern necessity. In Taniti, our power outlets are 120 volts, which is the same as in the United States!

Fishing Season

Fishing is a major part Taniti's agriculture and economy. Our world famous Fish Market is a unique shopping experience! Purchase fresh-caught fish, by the pound. Watch our island-renowned chefs put their skills to the test -- they may even ask you to try out their tasty creations and influence the next Weekly Special! Enjoy the various delicious menus while you shop. listen to live music, or watch the sailor's in the harbor bring in the day's catch!

Track our Events page for details on what to expect seasonally when participating in our fishing tours, snorkeling lessons, and what specials to expect in the Fish Market!

Necessities

For longer stays in Taniti, visitors have options of where to get their essentials! Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
*The drinking age on Taniti is 18 years-old and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.
*Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

In Taniti, your safety matters to us. We are proud to say violent crime on Taniti Island is very rare, but as tourism increases, there are more reports pickpocketing and other petty crimes. Keep your belongings in a hidden or hard-to-reach place such as a locked glove compartment in your car, or a zipped bag to carry with you. For emergency services, dial #---.

Hospital & Clinic

In Taniti, we take care of each other, so while you are here with us, we want you to have the resources necessary to stay healthy and active for exploring! Taniti has one major hospital to serve the island, plus several clinics for general or urgent care. The hospital has many multilingual employees, so no matter where you are visiting from, rest assured, you are never too far from a friend.
